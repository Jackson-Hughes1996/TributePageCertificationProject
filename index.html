Sly Cooper

Member of the illustrious Cooper thieving clan


Sly cooper silhouetted against the night sky with the moon in the middle, on the way to a heist no doubt.


Here are some notable events in Sly Cooper's life:


  • Sly Cooper And The Thievieus RaccoonusThe first videogame in the series where Sly and the gang are tasked with stealing back pages of the Thievieus Raccoonus taken by the Klaww gang and taking down it's leader, Clockwerk.

  • Sly 2: Band Of ThievesThe second videogame in the series where the Klaww gang once again steals the Clockwerk pieces thought destroyed in the first game. Sly and the gang have to take them back.
  • Sly 3: Honor Among ThievesThe final videogame in the series where Sly and his growing gang of thieves bust into the Cooper Vault on a Pacific island so that Sly can connect with his family's legacy and finally see the inside of this vault he had only heard about as a child. And write some pages of the Thievius Raccoonus of his own.
  • Sly 4: Thieves In Time
